Hello, I am Hanqi Jiang, currently an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University. Despite being an undergraduate, I have amassed extensive research experience, including internships at organizations like Baidu, China Telecom Beijing Research Institute, the State Key Laboratory of Computer Architecture at the Institute of Computing Technology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the School of Vehicle and Mobility at Tsinghua University, and the Multimedia Technology Lab at the School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research has delved into various areas including traditional vision tasks (segmentation, detection), robotics-related tasks (simulation, control), autonomous driving, and the emerging field of LLM (Large Language Model).
