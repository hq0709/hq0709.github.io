I am currently working as a research assistant at the Intelligent Multimedia Technology Laboratory, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Ruimao Zhang. My current research focus is the application of large language models (LLMs) in the field of healthcare, such as segmentation and pathological diagnosis. Prior to joining CUHK, I interned at the State Key Laboratory of Computer Architecture, Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I mainly researched 3D computer vision under the guidance of Prof. Yinhe Han. I am expected to receive a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Jiaotong University in the summer of 2024.

My chinese name is 蒋 瀚 祺 .
