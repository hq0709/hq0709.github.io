Biography

I am currently a first year PhD student at the University of Georgia. My research focus is medical image analysisthe and application of multi-modal large language models (MLLMs) in the field of healthcare. Prior to joining UGA, I interned at Li Auto, CUHKSZ, ICTCAS, Tsinghua University and Baidu. I am expected to receive a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Jiaotong University in the summer of 2024.
